The Monster Bed Willis J/Varley S Beaver

A humorous tale in rhyme of a monster who’s scared to go to sleep in case there are any humans hiding under his bed! Watch for the twist in the tail when a little boy unexpectedly arrives lost in the wood, having run away from school!

The Castle of Fear Burston Patrick Walker Books

An activity book where the child engages in a personal journey with choices. Good to stimulate fantasies and discussion

There’s a Sea in my Bedroom Wild Margaret Picture Puffins

David is frightened of the sea; until one day he enters a friendly sea world in his imagination through holding a shell to his ear – and he finds that its fun!

The Ghost Eye Tree Martin Junior Bill/Archambault John Picture Puffins

About bravery in the face of real fear. Helpful when used with care and thought – could evoke anxiety. Also touches on ridicule and support. Very evocative pictures – and story too!
